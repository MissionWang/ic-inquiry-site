Quality Control Center

Our in-house lab and local partnerships in Huaqiangbei ensure 100% genuine parts for your production line.

LEVEL 01

External Visual Inspection

Every batch undergoes rigorous visual analysis using high-resolution digital microscopes (up to 200x). Our specialists check:

  • Laser Marking consistency & Original Font verification
  • Package/Case integrity (No resurfacing or sanding marks)
  • Lead/Ball condition (Oxidation check & Co-planarity measurement)
  • Acetone/Solvent Test (Re-blacktop and remarking detection)
LEVEL 02

Non-Destructive X-Ray Inspection

Advanced real-time imaging to ensure internal silicon architecture matches the specification without opening the package.

  • Bond Wire Analysis (Check for consistency and broken wires)
  • Die Frame Verification (Matches manufacturer's lead-frame pattern)
  • XRF Analysis (Full RoHS Lead-Free compliance scan)

Professional Partnered Lab

For critical military, aerospace, and medical grade components, we partner with industry-leading 3rd party labs in Shenzhen to provide:

  • X-Ray Inspection (Internal Die Verification)
  • De-cap Testing (Internal Architecture Analysis)
  • Solderability Testing (Terminal Integrity)
  • Comprehensive Testing Reports (English PDF)
